according to Will

Top 3 movies of all time

1. V for Vendetta

Why I recommend it:

Stylistic and thought-provoking. V for Vendetta is a cautionary tale of totalitarianism in the not-so-distant future with epic fight sequences that remind you of the Matrix.

2. Spirited Away

Why I recommend it:

Nostalgic and breathtaking. A great introduction into the fantastical world of Hayao Miyazaki. An eleven year old girl forced by her parents to move to a rural countryside stumbles across an ancient bathhouse for spirits and other supernatural beings.

3. The Truman Show

Why I recommend it:

Tragic but funny. What if one day you discover your life is a TV show and you are its unwitting star? Jim Carrey delivers his comedic antics as a man and his gradual unravelling over the realization that nothing in his life is real.
