according to Will

Top 3 movies of all time

1. V for Vendetta

Why I recommend it:

Stylistic and thought-provoking. V for Vendetta is a cautionary tale of totalitarianism in the not-so-distant future with epic fight sequences that remind you of the Matrix.

2. Spirited Away

Why I recommend it:

Nostalgic and breathtaking. A great introduction into the fantastical world of Hayao Miyazaki from the eyes of an eleven year old girl who is forced by her parents to move to rural countryside and stumbles across an ancient bathhouse for spirits and other supernatural beings.

3. The Truman Show

Why I recommend it:

Tragic but funny. What if one day you discover your life is a TV show and you are its unwitting star? Jim Carrey delivers his usual comedic acting about a man's gradual unravelling over his realization that nothing in his life is real.
